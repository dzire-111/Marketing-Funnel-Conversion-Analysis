# Marketing Funnel & Conversion Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign data to understand how leads convert into customers. The goal is to evaluate marketing funnel performance, identify drop-off points, and determine which customer segments and channels generate higher conversion rates.

The analysis was conducted using Excel for data preparation and Power BI for building an interactive dashboard that highlights key marketing insights.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze marketing funnel performance
* Measure lead-to-customer conversion rates
* Identify high-performing marketing channels
* Compare conversion rates across customer segments
* Provide recommendations to improve campaign effectiveness

---

## Dataset

Dataset Used: Bank Marketing Dataset (UCI Machine Learning Repository)

The dataset contains records of marketing campaigns conducted by a bank. Each row represents a customer contacted during a marketing campaign.

Key variables include:

* Customer demographics (age, job, education, marital status)
* Contact method used in marketing campaigns
* Campaign timing (month)
* Previous marketing outcomes
* Customer conversion outcome

The column y indicates whether the customer subscribed to the product:

* `yes` → Converted customer
* `no` → No conversion

# Key Insights

1. Overall Conversion Rate
The campaign achieved a conversion rate of approximately **11.7%**, meaning around one out of every nine leads resulted in a successful customer conversion.

---

2. Contact Method Performance
Customers contacted through **cellular communication channels** generated the highest number of conversions, significantly outperforming telephone contacts.

---

3. Seasonal Campaign Trends
Marketing campaigns conducted during certain months generated higher conversion volumes, suggesting that campaign timing plays an important role in marketing success.

---

4. Customer Job Segments
Customers working in management, technician, and blue-collar professions showed higher conversion numbers compared to other job categories.

---

5. Education Level Influence
Customers with secondary and tertiary education levels demonstrated stronger response rates to marketing campaigns.

---

# Business Recommendations
Improve High-Performing Channels
Since cellular contact generated the highest conversions, marketing teams should prioritize mobile-based outreach strategies.

---

Optimize Campaign Timing
Campaign performance varies across months. Businesses should analyze historical data to launch campaigns during periods with higher engagement.

---

Focus on High-Conversion Customer Segments
Targeting job segments that historically show higher conversion rates (such as management and technicians) can improve marketing efficiency.

---

Refine Customer Targeting

Marketing efforts should focus on demographics that respond better to campaigns, such as customers with higher education levels.

---

Improve Lead Qualification

By analyzing customer profiles and campaign history, companies can prioritize higher quality leads, improving overall conversion efficiency.
